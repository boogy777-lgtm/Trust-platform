# Safety And Commissioning

If the plant question is about emergency stop handling, `e-stop` boundaries,
safe state, restart discipline, or commissioning sign-off, start here.

## E-Stop And Safe-State Boundary

Live plant use needs explicit safety claims, evidence, and site-owned
procedures outside the software.

Before I/O, deployment, or HMI operator rollout, the team needs a written
safe-state boundary, a restart procedure, an evidence bundle, and a sign-off
owner.
